About Vibiz Inc.

Transforming how businesses create and deploy marketing funnels through the power of conversational AI.

Our Mission

We believe that every business professional should have access to powerful marketing tools without the complexity. Our mission is to democratize funnel creation by making it as simple as having a conversation.

Why We Built Vibiz Inc.

The Problem

Traditional funnel builders require extensive technical knowledge, design skills, and weeks of work. Most businesses struggle to create effective marketing funnels that convert.

Our Solution

By leveraging advanced AI and natural language processing, we've created a platform that understands your business needs from a simple conversation and automatically generates everything you need.

The Result

Business professionals can now launch complete marketing campaigns in minutes instead of weeks, focusing on what matters most - growing their business.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Vibiz Inc. emerged from a simple observation: the best marketing insights come from conversations with customers, not from complex software interfaces. Our founders, with backgrounds in AI research and digital marketing, set out to bridge this gap.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of business professionals who use our platform to create high-converting funnels without the traditional barriers of time, cost, and complexity.
